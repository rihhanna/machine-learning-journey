## 🧠 Neurons and the Brain

Neural Networks are loosely inspired by biological neurons.

Just as neurons in the brain receive signals, process information, and produce outputs, artificial neurons receive inputs, perform calculations, and generate predictions.

However, neural networks are much simpler than real brains.

---

## 📊 Demand Prediction Example

One application of neural networks is demand prediction.

Examples:

* Predicting product sales
* Forecasting customer demand
* Estimating future business needs

Neural networks can learn complex patterns from historical data and make predictions about future outcomes.

---

## 🖼️ Image Recognition Example

Neural networks are widely used in image recognition.

Examples:

* Identifying objects in photos
* Face recognition
* Medical image analysis
* Self-driving cars

A neural network can learn patterns from pixels and recognize different objects automatically.

---

## 🎯 Key Idea

A neural network learns by:

1. Receiving inputs
2. Processing information through neurons
3. Producing outputs
4. Adjusting its parameters to improve predictions

The goal is to learn patterns from data and make accurate predictions.
