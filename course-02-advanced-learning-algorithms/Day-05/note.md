## Neural Network Training

Training is the process of teaching a neural network to learn from data.

The model:
1. Makes predictions
2. Calculates errors
3. Adjusts its parameters
4. Repeats the process until predictions improve

---

## TensorFlow Implementation

I learned how TensorFlow helps automate the training process.

TensorFlow provides tools for:

- Building neural networks
- Training models
- Making predictions
- Evaluating performance

---

## Training Details

A neural network learns by adjusting weights and biases.

The goal is to minimize error and improve prediction accuracy.

Training involves:
- Forward Propagation
- Cost Calculation
- Gradient Descent
- Parameter Updates
